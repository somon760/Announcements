Appendix

Here you will find references, special notes, and detailed information to supplement the campaign's progress and resources.

This appendix summarizes the loot and clues found by Team Biscuit during their adventures in Icewind Dale. These treasures and insights will prove crucial in their battle against Pengu and Keraptis, especially as they prepare to craft the Candle of the Dark One and confront the dangers that lie ahead in the Lost Spire of Netheril.

Appendix: Loot and Clues Discovered by Team Biscuit

Section 1: Loot Found Throughout the Adventure

1. Ring of Invisibility
  • Location: Discovered during The White Lady’s Ghost in Easthaven.
  • Description: A magical ring that allows the wearer to become invisible at will.
  • Discovery: Thunderdrum found this ring hidden among the treasures of the White Lady after the party laid her spirit to rest.
2. Potion of Giant Size
  • Location: Found during Foaming Mugs in Bryn Shander.
  • Description: Temporarily increases the drinker’s size and strength, granting giant-like abilities for a short duration.
  • Discovery: Hidden in a dwarven treasure stash beneath a tavern, discovered after clearing out a duergar smuggling operation.
3. Candle of the Dark One (Materials)
  • Location: Collected from various locations across Icewind Dale.
  • Description: Three Wicks and three Wax of the Dark One, enough to attempt the creation of the Candle of the Dark One three times.
  • Discovery: The party gathered the ingredients over the course of several adventures, including the Arcane Brotherhood and Frostmaiden’s Followers quests.
4. Shadowkissed Silk
  • Location: Obtained during the Arcane Brotherhood storyline.
  • Description: Used to craft the Wick of the Dark One, this rare material is imbued with shadow magic to bind malevolent energies.
  • Discovery: Discovered in the hidden chambers of an Arcane Brotherhood stronghold during their quest to find ingredients for the Candle of the Dark One.
5. Whispering Willow Wood
  • Location: Found during the Frostmaiden’s Followers and Frost Druid Cults quest.
  • Description: An enchanted wood used to craft the Wick of the Dark One, it resonates with whispers of ancient spirits.
  • Discovery: Retrieved from a sacred grove of corrupted frost druids, who were purged by the party in their quest to stop the Frost Druid cults.
6. Frostrazor (Legendary Weapon)
  • Location: Located in the Cave of the Berserkers, wielded by Grek the Frost Giant.
  • Description: A legendary weapon with the power to freeze enemies upon striking them, dealing immense cold damage and boosting the wielder’s frost-based abilities.
  • Discovery: Shrop learned of Frostrazor’s existence through a vision from Eldath, and the party tracked it to the Cave of the Berserkers. After defeating Grek and the Keraptis Clone, Frostrazor was claimed by Shrop.

Section 2: Clues Discovered Along the Way

1. Pengu’s Statue and Corruption
  • Location: Discovered through Grim’s research into the Arcane Brotherhood.
  • Clue: A massive Emperor Penguin statue to the north has corrupted all creatures within a 50-mile radius. This entity, Pengu, is siphoning magic from Auril to send waves of anti-magic across the Dale.
  • Discovery: The party first learned of Pengu through documents recovered from the Arcane Brotherhood, with further evidence encountered during their travels.
2. The Candle of the Dark One
  • Location: Learned during the Arcane Brotherhood storyline.
  • Clue: The Candle of the Dark One is the only item capable of binding Pengu and ending its influence over Icewind Dale. The party now possesses enough materials to make three attempts to craft the candle, but they have not yet attempted the ritual.
  • Discovery: The ritual’s details were found in forbidden texts hidden away by the Arcane Brotherhood, discovered after the team infiltrated their stronghold.
3. Connection Between Pengu and Auril
  • Location: Uncovered during the Frostmaiden’s Followers and Frost Druid Cults questline.
  • Clue: Pengu has been siphoning power from Auril, using it to amplify its own abilities. This results in the anti-magic waves that frequently disrupt the party’s spells.
  • Discovery: After defeating a group of Frostmaiden’s followers, Team Biscuit uncovered sacred texts that hinted at the relationship between Pengu and the Frostmaiden.
4. Keraptis and the Weapons of White Plume Mountain
  • Location: Alluded to through visions and ancient texts.
  • Clue: The Keraptis entity, believed to be the original, possesses all four legendary weapons: Wave, Whelm, Blackrazor, and Frostrazor. The party believes they may need these weapons to complete the Candle of the Dark One ritual and bind Pengu.
  • Discovery: Shrop and Terry received divine clues from their gods, Eldath and Pelor, hinting at Keraptis’s presence in the Cave of the Berserkers.
5. Eldath’s Blessing and Frostrazor
  • Location: Revealed to Shrop during his ice-fishing encounter in Easthaven.
  • Clue: Eldath revealed the location of Frostrazor and the presence of Keraptis in the Cave of the Berserkers.
  • Discovery: Shrop received a vision from Eldath while ice fishing, guiding the team toward their next destination.
6. The Emperor Penguin Corruption
  • Location: Discovered in Icewind Dale’s northern regions.
  • Clue: Creatures around the massive Emperor Penguin statue have been corrupted by Pengu’s dark magic. This corruption has spread over a 50-mile radius, influencing even the land itself.
  • Discovery: The party encountered this corruption firsthand when they investigated strange reports of behavior from local wildlife, eventually tracing it back to the statue during the Arcane Brotherhood questline.
7. Lost Spire of Netheril
  • Location: Revealed by the Keraptis Clone during the battle in the Cave of the Berserkers.
  • Clue: Before his death, the Keraptis Clone hinted that the remaining legendary weapons were hidden in the Lost Spire of Netheril.
  • Discovery: This was revealed by the clone during the final confrontation, pushing the team to find this mysterious location.
