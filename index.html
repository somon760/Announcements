Timeline of Quests Completed

  1. Fifth-day, 10th of Tarsakh, 1405
    • Toil and Trouble: Completed after retrieving the magical cauldron from the Frost Giants and returning it to Easthaven.
  2. Fifth-day, 24th of Tarsakh, 1405
    • The White Lady’s Ghost: The party laid the spirit of the White Lady to rest, discovering the Ring of Invisibility.
  3. Seventh-day, 2nd of Nightal, 1405
    • The Easthaven Ferry: Ensured the ferry’s continued operation and resolved the duergar threat in the town.
  4. Ninth-day, 16th of Nightal, 1405
    • Foaming Mugs (Bryn Shander): Completed this quest, discovering the Potion of Giant Size in the smuggling operation.
  5. Seventh-day, 23rd of Nightal, 1405
    • A Beautiful Mine (Termalaine): Resolved the mine’s corruption and helped the town return to stability.
  6. Fifth-day, 1st of Hammer, 1406
    • Investigate the Duergar Conspiracy: Stopped the duergar plot and thwarted their plans in Icewind Dale.
  7. Fifth-day, 10th of Hammer, 1406
    • Arcane Brotherhood: The party uncovered clues about Pengu, Auril, and the Candle of the Dark One during this storyline.
  8. Fifth-day, 24th of Hammer, 1406
    • Frostmaiden’s Followers and Frost Druid Cults: Defeated corrupted frost druids, uncovering the Whispering Willow Wood.
  9. Eighth-day, 30th of Hammer, 1406
    • Side Quests to Gather Intel: Completed several minor quests to gather information about the Frostmaiden’s followers.
  10. Fifth-day, 10th of Eleasis, 1406
    • Cave of the Berserkers: Defeated the Keraptis Clone and retrieved Frostrazor.
  11. Fifth-day, 14th of Eleasis, 1406
    • Returned to Easthaven after the Emperor Penguin raid. Now preparing to pursue the clues leading to the Lost Spire of Netheril.
