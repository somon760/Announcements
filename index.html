If a player continues leveling the same class through levels 21 to 49, they gain Epic Boons at levels 21, 25, 29, 31, 35, 39, 41, 45, and 49

Players may choose from any Official Epic Boons or choose one from the Custom Epic Boons** below.

** Warning: Some Epic Boons may have possible side-effects, or cause life, body and/or mind-altering effects.  Caution should be taken with some of the more powerful sounding Epic Boons as side-effects tend to scale in power with the Epic Boon.  As always, GM discretion applies.  Application of an Epic Boon could require in-game actions, hard to attain items or specific or difficult training and will be irreversable once achieved.  Be prepared to back up any Epic Boon request with a logical reason for why or how your character could attain such a power.  As always, more plausable and entertaining origin stories will be given greater consideration.
